Never Give Up

-Taniya

It's been since long we'r hearing that so and so committed suicide bcoz he /she could not reserve a seat in IIT or any other prestigious institute. I myself have failed JEE twice ,could not have admission in any good college but never considered myself a failure except what I learnt through all these years Is that never give up on urself... U'r life is far more important than this single JEE RESULT piece of paper....learn from ur failures learn from what adversities u'r facing.... Coming on most important aspect of our youth 'WHAT PEOPLE WOULD SAY ABOUT ME.... WILL THEY CALL ME A FAILURE ' I ask u let them say I was put down by people actually my close relatives who quoted ' TUM LIFE M KUCH NAHI KAR PAOGII ' I would not say it didn't bothered me.... It did but then I thought let's take these conversations in a positive way....i challenged myself that I'll shut all the mouths out there..... All I want to say is never take ur life for granted ..it's precious... We're blessed to be humans... Live... Rise.... Fall.... Learn but never give up.
